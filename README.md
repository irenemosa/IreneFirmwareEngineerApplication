Hello! My name is Irene Mollet, and I am an Embedded Systems Engineer with experience in firmware development, hardware/software integration, testing, validation, and technical support.

I created this application as part of my application for the Firmware Engineer position at Fliteboard. Rather than submitting only a traditional CV, I wanted to build something that better represents who I am as an engineer, how I approach projects, and the type of work I enjoy doing.

## Why I Built This Application

When I found the Firmware Engineer position at Fliteboard, I felt that the role aligned surprisingly well with both my professional interests and personal values.

Since I started my engineering career, I have always wanted to combine three things that motivate me the most:

- Creating innovative products
- Sports and outdoor activities
- Sustainability and positive impact

Instead of simply describing my experience, I decided to build a small application that allows you to explore my background, projects, skills, education, and interests in an interactive way.

## Why a Desktop Application?

Although web applications are more common for portfolios, I chose a desktop application as it aligns better with the Embedded systems role.

The project demonstrates software architecture, user interface development, and object-oriented programming concepts.

*From a personal perspective*:
I have always enjoyed desktop development. Visual Basic and .NET were among the first technologies I learned professionally, and they remain tools that I genuinely enjoy working with. While most of my embedded development experience is in C and C++, I appreciate the productivity, readability, and rapid prototyping capabilities that the .NET ecosystem provides. 

For this project, I selected .NET and Visual Basic because they provide a productive development environment while also offering the flexibility to integrate future projects and components developed in other languages, such as C and C++. This makes the application a scalable platform that can evolve together with my portfolio.
